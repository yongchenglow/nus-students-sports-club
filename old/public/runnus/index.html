About RunNUS

RunNUS is an annual NUS run event organized by NUS Students' Sports Club to promote healthy and active lifestyle among the NUS population and the public.

Since its inception in 2007, RunNUS has become one of the biggest and most anticipated NUS run for students, staff and alumni. Above running and fun, RunNUS works with NUS Disability Support Office (DSO) and Disabled People's Association (DPA) this year. With the tagline 'Run For A Cause', we aim to empower the community of People With Disabilities/Special Needs (PWDSNs) through inclusiveness.
