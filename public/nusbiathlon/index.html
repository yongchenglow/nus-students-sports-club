About Us
The most anticipated annual swim-and-run event
NUS Biathlon is an annual event organized by the NUS Students’ Sports Club (NUSSSC) to promote a healthy lifestyle amongst the NUS population as well as the public. NUS Biathlon is the first student-organized and student-led inter-varsity Aquathlon event in Singapore.
This year, NUS Biathlon focuses on attracting beginners to try out this endurance sport and to spark interest in endurance sports in the NUS community, as well as the public.
