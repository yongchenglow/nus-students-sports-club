Hello!

The elections for the 40th Management Committee of NUS Students’ Sports Club have commenced!

NUS Students’ Sports Club (NUSSSC) is the largest non-faculty constituent club in NUS. As one of the most active and wide-reaching student-run organisations on campus, we are dedicated to be the leading platform to promote community bonding through recreational sports.

To achieve this, we are a family of 18 Member Clubs (categorised into Martial Arts, Water, Land & Indoor sports) and 6 Sports Club Projects (NUS Biathlon, SunNUS, NUS Sports Camp, MADNUS, RunNUS and Inter-Faculty Games), providing the NUS population with an array of sporting activities and multiple channels to enrich their university experience.

The Management Committee oversees the operations of all Member Clubs and Sports Club Projects by providing administrative, financial and logistical support. As the bridge between Sports Club and the general NUS population, Office of Student Affairs (OSA) and external organisations, we are also able to strategically brand these entities and assist in maximising the impact of each of their events and operations.

The Management Committee

"If you want to change the world, find someone to help you paddle." ~ William H. McRaven

Presidential Cell

We oversee all the operations within Sports Club and act as the interface between Sports Club and the school. We also help to make strategic decisions, and influence school policies towards recreational sports in general.

Finance Cell

We oversee all finances under Sports Club and ensure that all financial procedures are carried out correctly.

Secretariat Cell

We help to settle necessary administrative, logistics and facilitate matters. We act as the interface with the sports office for the various sporting events and projects.

Marketing Cell

We help to secure strategic supports and privilege partners for Sports Club. We also manage the disbursement of marketing products.

Publicity Cell

We strategically brand Sports Club and its umbrella through the various outreach platforms available.

Welfare Cell

We organize various initiatives to enhance the well-being as well as appreciate the members for their commitments and hard work.

Projects Cell

We work together to strategically brand and organize the 6 Sports Club Projects, ensuring its quality and excellence.

NUSSU Exco Rep

We facilitate coordination and cooperation efforts between NUSSU Exco and Sports club and we represent the interest of the sporting community when engaging with the NUS administration.
