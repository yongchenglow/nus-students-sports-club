SPORTS CLUB PROJECTS

6 ANNUAL FLAGSHIP EVENTS

NUS Biathlon

Date: 23 March 2019

Biathlon is an annual swim and run event. It is open to public and we welcome everyone from newcomers to competitive athletes.

SunNUS

Date: 15 June 2019

SunNUS is the largest and most anticipated annual beach sporting event. With a thrilling run, team sports series and beach pageant, there is something for everyone.

NUS Sports Camp

Date: 1-4 July 2019

NUS Sports Camp is an annual freshman orientation camp. The 4D3N camp brings sports persons together and allows individuals to try out a wide array of sports.

IFG

Date: August-September 2019

IFG is a highly anticipated annual sporting event that brings together the 10 NUS faculties and Yale-NUS in a friendly competition that spans across 20 sports.

MadNUS

Date: August 2019

MADNUS (Martial Arts Day NUS) invites you to learn more about the 11 martial arts groups. Regardless of your background. Come down and discover the fighter in you.

RunNUS

Date: September 2019

RunNUS is an annual running event. Above fun and running, RunNUS is a partner of the NUS Disability Support Office.
